Frequently Asked Questions

What is Lorem Ipsum?

Lorem Ipsum is placeholder text widely used in the design and printing industry. It comes from a Latin text by Cicero written in 45 BC. It has been the industry standard placeholder text since the 1500s, used to demonstrate the visual form of a document without relying on meaningful content.

Why use Lorem Ipsum instead of real text?

Placeholder text lets designers focus on layout and typography without being distracted by readable content. Readers tend to focus on the meaning of readable text, which can obscure how the layout looks. Lorem Ipsum is deliberately meaningless so it doesn't draw attention away from design decisions.

What is the difference between paragraphs, sentences, and words?

Paragraphs generate full blocks of text (typically 5-8 sentences each), suitable for body copy areas. Sentences generate individual sentences for smaller text areas. Words generates a specified number of individual words, useful for headings or short labels.

Can I generate more than 50 units?

The tool supports up to 50 units (paragraphs, sentences, or words) per generation. For larger volumes, simply generate multiple times and combine the output as needed.

Is the generated text always the same?

The generator uses a standard Lorem Ipsum word pool and produces consistent, natural-looking placeholder text. The "Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet" opening is the traditional first paragraph of Lorem Ipsum and is optionally prepended when the checkbox is checked.
