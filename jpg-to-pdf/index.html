Frequently Asked Questions

What image formats are supported? +
JPG (JPEG) and PNG images are supported. WebP, GIF and other formats are not currently supported by the embedded pdf-lib library.
Is the image order preserved? +
Yes. Images appear in the PDF in the same order they were selected. On most operating systems you can select multiple files in order by holding Shift when clicking.
