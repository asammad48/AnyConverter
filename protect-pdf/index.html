Secure your PDF with a password so only authorised users can open it. The encryption is applied entirely in your browser — your file never leaves your device.

How to Password Protect a PDF

  1. Step 1 — Upload your PDF file.
  2. Step 2 — Enter and confirm your password (minimum 4 characters).
  3. Step 3 — Click "Protect & Download" to get the encrypted PDF.

Features

  • AES-based PDF password encryption
  • Password visibility toggle
  • Confirm password validation to prevent typos
  • 100% browser-based — your file never leaves your device

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I remove the password later? +
Yes. Use the Unlock PDF tool on this site to remove the password if you know the current one.
What encryption is used? +
The pdf-lib library applies RC4-based 128-bit encryption as supported by the PDF specification. For maximum security use a strong, unique password.
