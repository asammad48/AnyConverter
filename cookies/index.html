Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, which cookies AnyConverter uses, and how you can control them. For broader privacy information, see our Privacy Policy.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, understand how you use the site, and deliver relevant advertisements. Cookies are widely used and generally harmless.

Modern browsers also have localStorage and sessionStorage — similar mechanisms AnyConverter uses to remember your preferences (such as dark mode and language settings) without relying on traditional cookies.

Essential Cookies

Essential cookies are required for the website to function correctly. They store your preferences such as theme (dark/light mode) and language. These cookies do not track you across other websites and cannot be disabled without breaking your experience.

Analytics Cookies

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use AnyConverter — which tools are most popular, how long people stay, and where they come from. All data is anonymised and aggregated. We cannot identify individual users from this data.

You can opt out of Google Analytics across all websites using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Advertising Cookies

AnyConverter is free to use. To support hosting and development costs, we display advertisements via Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to show you ads based on your browsing history and interests.

You can manage your ad preferences at Google Ad Settings, or opt out of interest-based advertising at aboutads.info.

How to Control Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Here are links to cookie management instructions for major browsers:

  • Mozilla Firefox

Note: Disabling cookies may affect your experience on AnyConverter (e.g., your theme preference won't be saved).
