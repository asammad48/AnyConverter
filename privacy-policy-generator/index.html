Frequently Asked Questions

Is this legally binding? +
This tool generates a template privacy policy for informational purposes. While it covers common privacy policy requirements, you should have it reviewed by a legal professional to ensure compliance with applicable laws such as GDPR, CCPA, or other regional regulations.
Do I need a privacy policy for my website? +
In most jurisdictions, yes — especially if you collect any personal data (even just analytics data or email addresses). Laws like GDPR (EU), CCPA (California), and PIPEDA (Canada) require a privacy policy if you process personal data of users in those regions.
Can I edit the generated policy? +
Absolutely. Copy the generated text and paste it into any text editor or your website's CMS. You can freely edit, expand, or customise any section to fit your specific use case.
