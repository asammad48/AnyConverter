Sticky Notes Online

Create digital sticky notes in your browser. Choose a colour, type your note, and it saves automatically. Perfect for quick reminders and ideas.

How to Use Sticky Notes

  1. Step 1 — Choose a colour from the dropdown.
  2. Step 2 — Click + Add Note to create a new sticky note.
  3. Step 3 — Click on the note text area and type your content.
  4. Step 4 — Click the × button on a note to delete it.

Features

  • 6 colour options: Yellow, Green, Blue, Pink, Purple, Rose
  • Editable in-place using contenteditable
  • Auto-saves to localStorage on every keystroke
  • Notes persist between sessions
  • No limit on number of notes

Frequently Asked Questions

Are my notes private? +
Yes. Notes are stored only in your browser's localStorage. They are never sent to any server. Only you can see them on this device.
Can I change a note's colour after creating it? +
Not currently — colour is set when you click Add Note. To change it, delete the note and create a new one with the desired colour.
