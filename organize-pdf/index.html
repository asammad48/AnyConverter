Organize PDF Pages

Reorder pages in your PDF to any custom sequence. Enter the new order (e.g. 3, 1, 2) and download the reorganised PDF instantly — all in your browser.

Drop your PDF here or click to browse

You can repeat page numbers to duplicate pages, or omit numbers to remove them.

How to Organise PDF Pages

  1. Step 1 — Upload your PDF file.
  2. Step 2 — The current page order is filled in automatically. Edit it to your desired sequence.
  3. Step 3 — Click "Reorganise & Download" to get the reordered PDF.

Features

  • Reorder pages to any custom sequence
  • Duplicate pages by repeating a page number
  • Remove pages by omitting them from the sequence
  • Supports PDFs of any size and page count
  • 100% private — processed entirely in your browser

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I duplicate a page? +
Yes. Simply enter a page number more than once in the sequence (e.g. 1, 2, 2, 3) and it will appear multiple times in the output.
Is the original file modified? +
No. Your original file is never uploaded or modified. All processing happens in your browser using the pdf-lib library.
