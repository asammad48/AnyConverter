Meeting Timer Online

Create a timed agenda for your meeting. Each item counts down automatically and advances to the next topic when time is up — keeping everyone on schedule.

    How to Use the Meeting Timer

    1. Step 1 — Add agenda items with a title and duration in minutes.
    2. Step 2 — Click Start Meeting to begin timing the first item.
    3. Step 3 — The timer auto-advances when each item's time runs out.
    4. Step 4 — Click Skip to Next to move on early, or Reset to start over.

    Features

    • Agenda items with name and duration
    • Auto-advances to next item when time expires
    • Progress bar turns red when under 20% time remaining
    • Skip button to move ahead manually
    • Agenda saved to localStorage between visits

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Can I reorder agenda items? +
    Not by dragging — delete and re-add items in the order you want. Items are processed in the order they appear in the list.
    Does the agenda save between sessions? +
    Yes. The agenda items are saved to localStorage so they're still there when you return. Use Reset to clear the timer state without losing your agenda.
