Frequently Asked Questions

What is a good internet speed? +
For basic browsing and email, 10 Mbps download is sufficient. For HD streaming, 25 Mbps is recommended. For 4K streaming or gaming, 50–100 Mbps is ideal. For households with multiple users, 100+ Mbps is recommended.
What is ping/latency? +
Ping measures the round-trip time for a small packet to travel from your device to the test server and back. Lower is better. Under 20ms is excellent, 20–50ms is good, 50–100ms is average, and above 100ms may cause noticeable delays in gaming or video calls.
Why might results differ from my ISP's advertised speed? +
Advertised speeds are theoretical maximums. Actual speeds depend on network congestion, distance from the server, your router, and the number of devices sharing your connection.
