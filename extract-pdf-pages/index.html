Pull out specific pages from a PDF and save them as a standalone PDF file. Enter individual pages or ranges — the extraction happens instantly in your browser.

How to Extract PDF Pages

  1. Step 1 — Upload your PDF.
  2. Step 2 — Enter the pages you want to keep (e.g. 1, 3-5).
  3. Step 3 — Click "Extract Pages & Download" to get the new PDF.

Features

  • Extract single pages or page ranges
  • Output pages in the exact order you specify
  • Works with any size PDF up to browser memory limits
  • No file upload — 100% private and browser-based

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between Extract and Split? +
Extract lets you pick specific pages to save as one new PDF. Split divides a PDF into multiple separate files at every page boundary or at specified points.
Can I extract non-consecutive pages? +
Yes. Enter them separated by commas — for example: 1, 4, 7, 10. The output PDF will contain those pages in the order you listed them.
