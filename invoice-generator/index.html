Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a watermark on the PDF? +
No. The downloaded PDF has no watermark, no branding from AnyConverter, and no advertisements. It is a plain professional invoice you can send directly to clients.
Can I add multiple line items? +
Yes. Click "+ Add Row" to add as many line items as you need. Each row has a description, quantity, and rate. The subtotal, tax, and total update automatically.
What currency symbols are supported? +
Any text up to 3 characters works as the currency symbol. Type $, €, £, ¥, kr, or any other currency symbol in the Currency Symbol field.
Is my invoice data stored anywhere? +
No. Everything runs in your browser. Your invoice data (names, amounts, items) is never sent to any server and is lost when you close the page. Download the PDF before closing.
