How to Roll Dice

  1. Step 1 — Click a quick-roll button (d4 through d20) for an instant single roll.
  2. Step 2 — Or set the number of dice and sides in the custom fields and click Roll!
  3. Step 3 — Each die result is shown as a tile. The total appears below for multiple dice.
  4. Step 4 — The last 10 rolls are shown in the history section below.

Features

  • Quick-roll buttons for all standard polyhedral dice (d4–d20)
  • Custom dice: any number of sides from 2 to 100
  • Roll up to 20 dice at once with sum total
  • d6 results shown as pip faces ⚀–⚅
  • Last 10 rolls tracked in roll history

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I roll non-standard dice like d3 or d100? +
Yes. Use the custom roll fields and enter any number of sides between 2 and 100. A d100 (percentile die) is entered as 100 sides.
Is this good for D&D or other RPGs? +
Absolutely. All standard RPG dice are available as quick-roll buttons. You can also roll multiple dice at once to simulate damage rolls like 2d6+3 (roll 2 dice, add 3 manually).
