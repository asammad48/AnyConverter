Privacy & Security
Yes, your data is completely safe. All processing happens entirely in your browser on your own device. Your files, text, and data are never sent to our servers. We literally cannot see what you're working on, even if we wanted to. You can verify this by watching your browser's network tab — no file upload requests are made.
We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics — things like "how many people visited the PDF Merge page today." We cannot identify individual users. For full details, see our Privacy Policy.
Yes. Because all processing is done locally in your browser, even sensitive documents (contracts, financial records, medical files) never leave your device. The risk profile is similar to using a desktop application on your own computer.
Technical
AnyConverter works on all modern browsers:
  • Google Chrome 90+
  • Mozilla Firefox 88+
  • Apple Safari 14+
  • Microsoft Edge 90+
  • Opera 76+
We recommend keeping your browser updated for the best experience and security.
Most tools work offline once the page has loaded, since all processing is done in your browser. However, a few tools require live data — Currency Converter (exchange rates), IP Address Lookup (geolocation), and Internet Speed Test — and need an active internet connection.
Since all processing happens in your browser, the only limit is your device's available memory. Most modern computers handle files up to several hundred megabytes without issues. Very large files (over 500MB) may be slow on older devices.
All our tools are browser-based applications powered by JavaScript. JavaScript is what makes the processing happen on your device instead of on a server. Without JavaScript enabled, the tools cannot function. All modern browsers have JavaScript enabled by default.
