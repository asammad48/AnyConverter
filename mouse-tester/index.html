How to Use

  1. Move your mouse over the purple test area to see coordinates.
  2. Left-click, right-click, or middle-click — each registers separately.
  3. Click dots fade out to show your click pattern clearly.
  4. Use the scroll wheel and see the direction detected.

Features

  • Tracks left, middle, and right mouse buttons separately
  • Shows real-time X/Y coordinates
  • Visual click dot trail with fade animation
  • Scroll wheel direction detection
  • Works with any mouse or trackpad

Frequently Asked Questions

Why doesn't my right-click register? +
The tool suppresses the context menu and captures the right mouse button event. If it still doesn't register, your mouse's right button may be damaged or the browser may have a conflicting extension.
Can I test mouse DPI? +
This tool tracks pixel-level movement within the test area. While it doesn't report DPI directly, you can judge sensitivity by how far the cursor moves compared to your physical movement across the test zone.
