How to Remove PDF Pages

  1. Step 1 — Upload your PDF file.
  2. Step 2 — Enter the pages you want to delete (e.g. 2, 4-6).
  3. Step 3 — Click "Remove Pages & Download".

Features

  • Remove individual pages or ranges (e.g. 3-7)
  • Mix ranges and individual pages in one input
  • Remaining pages keep their original order
  • 100% browser-based — your file never leaves your device

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I remove multiple ranges at once? +
Yes. Enter multiple ranges and pages separated by commas — for example: 1, 3-5, 8, 10-12.
What happens if I try to remove all pages? +
The tool will prevent you from removing all pages, as a PDF must contain at least one page.
