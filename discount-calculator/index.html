How to Use Discount Calculator

  1. Step 1 — Choose your calculation mode: "Discount %" to apply a percentage off, "Fixed Discount" to subtract a dollar amount, or "Find Discount %" to discover the percentage from two prices.
  2. Step 2 — Enter the required price values in the input fields provided for your chosen mode.
  3. Step 3 — Click "Calculate" to instantly see the sale price, your savings, and the percentage off.

Features

  • Three calculation modes for different discount scenarios

  • Handles percentage discounts from 0% to 100%
  • Fixed dollar discount mode for coupon-style savings
  • Reverse mode to find the original discount % from any two prices
  • One-click reset to clear all fields and start fresh

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate a discount percentage? +
Use the "Discount %" mode. Enter the original price and the discount percentage. The calculator multiplies the price by the percentage and subtracts it to give you the sale price and your total savings.
What is the "Find Discount %" mode? +
This mode lets you discover what percentage discount was applied when you know both the original price and the sale price. For example, if an item was $100 and is now $75, the tool will calculate that as a 25% discount.
Can I calculate multiple discounts stacked together? +
For stacked discounts, apply the first discount to get an intermediate price, then use that result as the original price for the second discount. Stacked discounts are not simply additive — a 20% and 10% discount is not 30% off.
How does the fixed discount mode work? +
Enter the original price and a fixed dollar discount amount (like a $15-off coupon). The calculator subtracts the fixed amount and also shows you the equivalent percentage discount you received.
Is this calculator free to use? +
Yes, the discount calculator is completely free to use with no signup, no login, and no limits. All calculations happen instantly in your browser with no data sent to any server.
