Decision Maker Online

Can't choose? Enter your options and hit Decide. The tool rapidly cycles through all choices before landing on one — so you can stop overthinking and start doing.

How to Use the Decision Maker

  1. Step 1 — Enter your options in the text area, one per line.
  2. Step 2 — Click Decide for me!
  3. Step 3 — Watch the display rapidly cycle through your options.
  4. Step 4 — The animation slows and lands on the chosen option.

Features

  • Animated cycling through all options before deciding
  • Speed gradually decreases for dramatic effect
  • Works with 2 or more options
  • No equal weighting bias — each option has equal probability
  • Instant results with no server round-trip

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the decision truly random? +
Yes. Even though the animation cycles through options in a visible way, the final winner is chosen with Math.random() before the animation starts — the animation is purely cosmetic.
What if I only have two options? +
The Decision Maker works with as few as 2 options. For a simple two-way choice you might also prefer the Coin Flip tool.
