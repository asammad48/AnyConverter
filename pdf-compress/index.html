Frequently Asked Questions

How much can PDF compression reduce file size? +
Results vary by PDF content. PDFs with many images can often be reduced by 30–70%. Text-heavy PDFs with little redundancy may see smaller reductions.
Will compression affect PDF quality? +
Low compression has virtually no visible quality impact. Higher compression levels remove more data, which can affect image sharpness slightly. Text is never degraded.
What if the compressed PDF is larger than the original? +
This can happen with already-optimized PDFs. If the compressed file is larger, download the original instead — this tool will warn you in that case.
Are my PDFs uploaded to a server? +
No. All compression happens entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Your PDF files never leave your device.
What is the maximum file size supported? +
There is no hard limit, but very large PDFs (over 100MB) may be slow to process depending on your device. For most documents (under 50MB), processing is fast.
