Rotate PDF Pages

Fix the orientation of your PDF pages. Rotate all pages or specific pages by 90°, 180°, or 270° — clockwise or counter-clockwise. Free and instant.

Drop your PDF here or click to browse

Leave "Pages" blank or type all to rotate every page. Or specify pages/ranges.

How to Rotate PDF Pages

  1. Step 1 — Upload your PDF.
  2. Step 2 — Choose the rotation angle (90°, 180°, or 270°).
  3. Step 3 — Specify which pages to rotate or leave blank for all pages.
  4. Step 4 — Click "Rotate & Download".

Features

  • Rotate all pages or specific pages/ranges
  • Three rotation options: 90° CW, 90° CCW, 180°
  • Rotation is additive (stacks with existing page rotation)
  • 100% browser-based — no server upload

Frequently Asked Questions

Why does "90° Clockwise" show 270° internally? +
PDF rotation is counter-clockwise by the PDF spec. So 90° clockwise visually equals adding 270° to the internal rotation value. The tool handles this automatically.
Can I rotate only landscape pages? +
Yes — manually enter the page numbers of your landscape pages in the "Pages" field. You can identify them by opening the PDF in a viewer first.
