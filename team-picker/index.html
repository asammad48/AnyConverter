How to Pick Teams

  1. Step 1 — Enter one name per line in the text area.
  2. Step 2 — Set the number of teams you want.
  3. Step 3 — Keep "Shuffle names" checked for random assignment (or uncheck to keep list order).
  4. Step 4 — Click Pick Teams to see colour-coded team cards.

Features

  • Round-robin distribution ensures balanced team sizes
  • Optional shuffle for truly random assignment
  • Up to 8 distinct team colours
  • Team member count shown per card
  • Works with any number of people and teams

Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if the number of people doesn't divide equally? +
The tool distributes members round-robin so some teams will have one more member than others. The team cards show the member count so you can see the distribution.
Can I keep the same teams by refreshing? +
No — the shuffle is re-run each time you click Pick Teams. If you want to keep results, screenshot the page or copy the team cards before clicking again.
