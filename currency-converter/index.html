How to Use Currency Converter

  1. Step 1 — Enter the amount you want to convert in the "Amount" field.
  2. Step 2 — Select the source currency in the "From" dropdown and the target currency in "To".
  3. Step 3 — Click "Convert" or wait for automatic conversion. Use the swap button (⇄) to reverse currencies.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the exchange rates? +
Rates are sourced from open.er-api.com, which updates daily using data from the European Central Bank and other financial sources. For everyday conversions they are highly accurate; for large financial transactions always verify with your bank or broker.
How many currencies are supported? +
The converter supports over 150 currencies including all major world currencies such as USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, AUD, CAD, CHF, CNY, INR, and many more exotic and regional currencies.
Do I need to create an account to use this? +
No account or signup is required. The currency converter is completely free to use directly in your browser with no registration needed.
What does the swap button do? +
The ⇄ swap button instantly switches the "From" and "To" currencies and fetches fresh rates for the new base currency. This is useful when you want to convert back in the other direction without manually changing the dropdowns.
How often are exchange rates updated? +
Exchange rates are updated once daily. The exact timestamp of the last update is displayed below the convert button after rates are loaded. You can click "Refresh Rates" at any time to fetch the latest available rates.
