How to Fill a PDF Form

  1. Step 1 — Upload a PDF with fillable form fields (AcroForm).
  2. Step 2 — The tool detects all fields and displays inputs for each one.
  3. Step 3 — Fill in the values, then click "Save Filled Form & Download".

Features

  • Automatically detects all AcroForm fields
  • Supports text fields, checkboxes and dropdowns
  • Option to flatten (lock) fields in the output
  • Works with any standard fillable PDF
  • 100% browser-based — your data never leaves your device

Frequently Asked Questions

My PDF says it has no form fields — why? +
Not all PDFs have interactive form fields (AcroForms). If the fields appear as text printed on the PDF rather than interactive inputs, the form is not fillable. Use the Edit PDF tool to overlay text instead.
What does "Flatten form" mean? +
Flattening merges the form fields into the page content, making them look the same but no longer editable. This prevents recipients from changing the values after you submit.
