How to Redact a PDF

  1. Step 1 — Upload your PDF. The first page dimensions are shown.
  2. Step 2 — Enter the page number, X/Y coordinates, width and height for each redaction.
  3. Step 3 — Click "+ Add Redaction" to add it to the list. Repeat for more areas.
  4. Step 4 — Click "Apply Redactions & Download".

Features

  • Add unlimited redaction rectangles across any pages
  • Remove individual redactions before applying
  • Coordinates in PDF points (origin bottom-left)
  • Redactions are solid black rectangles drawn on the page
  • 100% browser-based — file stays on your device

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find the coordinates of text I want to redact? +
Open the PDF in Adobe Acrobat and hover over the area — it shows coordinates in the status bar. Alternatively use the PDF to JPG tool to get an image and measure pixel positions (remembering that 1 pt ≈ 1 px at 72 DPI).
Is the underlying text truly removed? +
No. This tool draws opaque black rectangles over the content — it does not remove the underlying text from the PDF structure. For legally compliant redaction, specialist software that permanently removes content is required.
