How to Use

  1. Click Start Camera — your browser will ask for camera permission.
  2. Grant permission to see your live webcam feed.
  3. Resolution, FPS, and device name appear automatically.
  4. Click 📸 Snapshot to download a still image.

Features

  • Live webcam preview directly in the browser
  • Shows resolution, frame rate, and device label
  • One-click snapshot download as PNG
  • No video is uploaded — everything stays on your device
  • Works with built-in and external webcams

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my video sent to any server? +
No. The webcam feed is processed entirely in your browser using the WebRTC MediaStream API. No video data is transmitted to any server.
The camera isn't working — what should I do? +
Ensure no other application is using the camera. Check that your browser has camera permission in your OS privacy settings. Try refreshing the page and granting permission again.
