Frequently Asked Questions

Which image formats are supported? +
You can convert from PNG, JPG, JPEG, WebP, AVIF, BMP and GIF. Output formats are PNG, JPG, WebP and BMP. AVIF support depends on your browser.
Is the conversion private? +
Yes. Your image is never uploaded to any server. All conversion happens locally in your browser using the Canvas API.
What quality setting should I use for JPG? +
For most photos, 80–90% quality provides an excellent balance of file size and visual quality. Use 100% for maximum quality or lower values for smaller file sizes.
Why is WebP better than JPG? +
WebP typically produces files 25–35% smaller than equivalent JPG at the same quality. It also supports transparency like PNG. All modern browsers support WebP.
Can I resize the image? +
Yes. Enter a width and/or height in pixels. With "Keep aspect ratio" checked, the other dimension adjusts automatically to maintain proportions.
