Spin the Wheel

Enter your options (one per line), spin the wheel and let randomness decide. Great for picking teams, choosing restaurants, assigning tasks or settling debates.

Advertisement

How to Spin the Wheel

  1. Step 1 — Enter your options in the text area, one per line.
  2. Step 2 — The wheel updates live as you type.
  3. Step 3 — Click Spin! to start the spinning animation.
  4. Step 4 — The winning option is announced when the wheel stops.

Features

  • Canvas-based animated spinning wheel
  • Smooth ease-out animation using requestAnimationFrame
  • Up to any number of options — wheel segments scale automatically
  • 8 distinct segment colours
  • Pointer on the right side indicates the winning segment

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the spin truly random? +
Yes. The final angle is determined by Math.random() which generates a cryptographically unpredictable value in modern browsers. Each option has an equal probability of winning.
How many options can I add? +
There's no hard limit. Practically, 2–20 options give the best visual result. With many options, labels may be shortened to fit within their segments.
