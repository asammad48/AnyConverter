Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I use fancy text? +
Fancy text works anywhere that renders Unicode, including Instagram bios, Twitter/X posts, Facebook, TikTok, Discord, LinkedIn, and most chat apps. Some platforms may restrict Unicode in certain fields.
Why do some letters look different or missing? +
Fancy text uses Unicode mathematical and letterlike symbols. If a character isn't in the Unicode mapping (like special punctuation), it's left unchanged. Some platforms may not display all Unicode ranges correctly.
Is this the same as a font? +
No. These aren't actual fonts — they are specific Unicode characters that visually resemble bold, italic, etc. text. The benefit is they work as plain text in any input field without needing font support.
Does it work with numbers and symbols? +
Bold, Double Struck, Monospace, and Circled styles support digits. Most styles map only standard Latin letters (A-Z, a-z). Symbols, punctuation, and accented characters are passed through unchanged.
