How to Compare Two PDFs

  1. Step 1 — Upload Document A (the original or baseline).
  2. Step 2 — Upload Document B (the revised version).
  3. Step 3 — Use the ← / → buttons to navigate pages side by side.
  4. Step 4 — Adjust zoom scale for finer detail.

Features

  • Side-by-side rendering of two PDFs
  • Synchronised page navigation
  • Load either or both PDFs — rendering starts as soon as one is loaded
  • 3 zoom scales: 1×, 1.5×, 2×
  • 100% browser-based via PDF.js — no upload required

Frequently Asked Questions

Does this highlight differences automatically? +
No. This tool provides a visual side-by-side view for manual comparison. Automatic difference highlighting (pixel-level diffing) requires server-side image processing not available in the browser.
Can the two PDFs have different page counts? +
Yes. The navigation goes up to the maximum page count of the two documents. When one document has fewer pages, its canvas will stop rendering at its last page.
