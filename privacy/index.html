1. Information We Collect

AnyConverter collects very minimal information. We do not collect:

  • Files you process using our tools
  • Text you enter into any tool
  • Passwords, documents, images, or any user-generated content
  • Account credentials (we have no accounts)

We may receive the following information automatically when you visit our website:

  • Log data — Your browser type, device type, the page you visited, and the time of your visit. This is standard server-side access logging retained for up to 30 days for security purposes.
  • Analytics data — Aggregated, anonymised usage statistics (pages visited, session duration) via Google Analytics. This data cannot identify you personally.

2. How Our Tools Work

All AnyConverter tools are client-side applications. This means every computation — formatting, converting, compressing, calculating — happens inside your web browser on your device.

When you upload a file to a PDF tool, format JSON, or generate a QR code, that data never leaves your computer. There is no back-end server that processes your content. Your data is processed by JavaScript running locally in your browser tab.

You can verify this by disabling your internet connection after a tool page has loaded — most tools will continue to function normally.

3. Cookies & Tracking

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies — Required for the website to function (e.g., dark mode preference, language preference). These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies — Google Analytics sets cookies to measure anonymous usage statistics. You can opt out via Google's opt-out tool.
  • Advertising cookies — Google AdSense may set cookies to display relevant advertisements. You can manage these through Google's Ad Settings.

For full details, see our Cookie Policy.

4. Third-Party Services

AnyConverter uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Analytics — Website analytics. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdSense — Advertising. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts — Font loading via CDN. Google Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare — CDN and DDoS protection. Cloudflare Privacy Policy

We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties beyond what is described above.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights under GDPR, CCPA, or other applicable laws:

  • Right to access — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to erasure — Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to object — Object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Right to data portability — Receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to withdraw consent — Withdraw consent for analytics/advertising at any time

Since we collect minimal data, most requests can be fulfilled immediately. To exercise your rights, contact us.

6. Data Retention

We retain server access logs for up to 30 days for security purposes. Aggregated, anonymised analytics data may be retained indefinitely. Since we do not collect personal data, there is nothing else to retain or delete.

7. Children's Privacy

AnyConverter is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of AnyConverter after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
