Habit Tracker Online

Build and maintain daily habits with a visual weekly grid. Track streaks, see your completion rate for the week and stay consistent.

How to Use the Habit Tracker

  1. Step 1 — Type a habit name and click Add Habit.
  2. Step 2 — Click the circle for each day you completed the habit this week.
  3. Step 3 — Watch the streak counter grow with consecutive daily completions.
  4. Step 4 — Delete a habit with the × button on the right.

Features

  • Weekly grid (Mon–Sun) for the current week
  • Streak counter — tracks consecutive days
  • Done/7 counter for weekly completion rate
  • Today's date is highlighted with a ring
  • Data persists in localStorage between visits

Frequently Asked Questions

Does it track habits across multiple weeks? +
Yes. The check data is stored by date, so you can always come back and it will show the correct week. The display always shows the current week, and the streak counts consecutive days going back from today.
Can I mark past days as done? +
Yes. Click any day circle — including past days — to toggle it on or off. The streak will be recalculated from today backwards.
