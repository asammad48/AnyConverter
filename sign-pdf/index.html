How to Sign a PDF

  1. Step 1 — Upload your PDF.
  2. Step 2 — Draw your signature in the canvas (mouse or touch).
  3. Step 3 — Choose the page, width and position sliders.
  4. Step 4 — Click "Embed Signature & Download".

Features

  • Draw signature with mouse or touch/stylus
  • Choose any page to place the signature on
  • Adjustable signature width and position (H/V sliders)
  • Signature embedded as a transparent PNG image
  • 100% browser-based — your file never leaves your device

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a legally binding signature? +
This tool creates a visual representation of a handwritten signature. Whether it constitutes a legally binding e-signature depends on your jurisdiction. For legally compliant e-signatures, a dedicated service with audit trail is recommended.
Can I reuse my signature? +
Currently the canvas is cleared each session. To reuse a signature, draw it once and apply it to all PDFs you need to sign in the same browser session.
