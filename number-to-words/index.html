Frequently Asked Questions

What is the largest number supported? +
The converter supports integers up to 999,999,999,999,999 (999 trillion). Numbers beyond this range will show an error message.
Does it handle decimal numbers? +
The converter rounds decimal numbers to the nearest integer before conversion. For example, 3.7 becomes "four" and 3.2 becomes "three". For precise decimal conversion, enter a whole number.
What is an ordinal number? +
An ordinal number indicates position or rank: first, second, third, etc. The tool automatically shows the ordinal suffix (st/nd/rd/th) alongside the word form — useful for dates and rankings.
Why does it use "and" in some numbers? +
The phrasing "one hundred and twenty-three" follows British English convention, which inserts "and" after the hundreds. American English omits the "and" — both are commonly accepted.
